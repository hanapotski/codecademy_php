Comparisons
So far we've seen:

strings (e.g. "dogs go woof!")
numbers (e.g. 4, 10)
Now let's learn about comparison operators.

List of comparison operators:

> Greater than
< Less than
<= Less than or equal to
>= Greater than or equal to
== Equal to
!= Not equal to
Instructions
